ANNUAL SECURITY AND FIRE SAFETY REPORT
The University of New Haven's Annual Security and Fire Safety Report is available for public review and inspection. The Jeanne Clery Disclosure of Campus Security Policy and Campus Crime Statistics Act (Clery Act) is a federal law that requires all colleges and universities to annually disclose information about crime on and around their campuses. A paper copy will be provided upon request. Requests can be made to the Department of Public Safety at (203) 932-7013.

Become a Charger: Apply for Admission
With over 68,000 alumni and over 100 years of history, #ChargerNation is the place to be. With a streamlined application process, competitive tuition and financial aid, as well as strong academic programs, applying couldn’t be easier. Whether currently in high school, want to transfer, or are looking for graduate school the University of New Haven has it all.

Admissions
----------
- The application deadline for Fall 2024 is July 1, 2024.
- Applications for Spring 2024 are accepted until November 1, 2023.
- International students must submit TOEFL or IELTS scores.

Academic Programs
------------------
- The Computer Science program offers both undergraduate and graduate degrees.
- Business Administration is available as a part-time and full-time MBA.
- The University offers a 5-year accelerated BS/MS program in Criminal Justice.

Tuition and Financial Aid
--------------------------
- Tuition for undergraduate programs is $40,000 per year.
- Graduate program tuition varies by department but averages $15,000 per semester.
- Financial aid is available through FAFSA for eligible students.
- Scholarships are available for both domestic and international students.

Campus Life
-----------
- The university has over 50 student clubs and organizations.
- Campus housing includes traditional dormitories and apartment-style residences.
- The main dining hall offers vegan, vegetarian, and halal meal options.

Events and Calendar
--------------------
- Orientation for new students will be held on August 25, 2024.
- The Fall semester begins on September 4, 2024.
- Career fairs are hosted twice a year in October and March.
